The Protocol Play

ARDP is the infrastructure bet.

Out-of-Home advertising is moving to programmatic, AI-driven buying — and there is currently no standard for how AI agents talk to DOOH infrastructure. Billbords designed one. Open specification, proprietary hub. Platform adapters at the top, screen networks at the bottom — scalable onboarding at every layer.

The Analogy

"Think what HTTP was for the web. That's what ARDP is for DOOH."

Platform Adapters — DoohClick, Broadsign and other SSPs/DSPs. Few, technical, high-leverage.

Screen Networks — Any network onboards without new code. Scales without Billbords involvement.

AI Agents — Customers connect via MCP registration + OAuth 2.1 at billbords.com/ardp.

Proprietary Hub — Open spec, but the routing and authentication infrastructure belongs to Billbords.
